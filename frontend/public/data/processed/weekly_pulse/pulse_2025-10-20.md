# Weekly Product Pulse: Oct 20-26
_Week: 2025-10-20 - 2025-10-26_

This week's product pulse highlights critical functional issues and user experience feedback. Key areas include user login problems and UI/UX concerns regarding portfolio clarity and feature requests for direct trading integration and a 'kill switch'. These insights are crucial for product improvements and strategic planning.

## Themes
- **Slow, Glitches** - A critical functional issue emerged as users reported being unable to log in to the application.
- **UI/UX** - Users expressed interest in new features like direct trading integration and a 'kill switch', alongside concerns regarding the clarity of the current portfolio visualization.

## Quotes
- "Slow, Glitches: why I am not able to login growws application"
- "UI/UX: trade directly in trading view to place order"
- "UI/UX: Portfolio is shown as dots in this app. No clarity at all."

## Actions
- Investigate and resolve the critical login failure reported by users.
- Prioritize research into the feasibility and user demand for direct trading integration and a 'kill switch' feature.
- Review and enhance the clarity of portfolio visualization, specifically addressing feedback regarding the 'dots' representation.
