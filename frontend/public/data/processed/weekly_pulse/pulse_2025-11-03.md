# Weekly Product Pulse: Key User Feedback & App Performance (Nov 3-9, 2025)
_Week: 2025-11-03 - 2025-11-09_

For Nov 3-9, 2025, user feedback highlights critical app stability and financial trust issues. Primary concerns include severe performance glitches causing financial losses, high and opaque fees, and declining UI/UX quality post-updates.

## Themes
- **Glitches** - Negative sentiment surrounds critical app malfunctions, including freezing, duplicate orders, and data inaccuracies, directly causing user financial losses.
- **Fees & Financial Concerns** - Users express strong dissatisfaction with high, increasing, and non-transparent charges, reporting unexpected deductions that erode investments.
- **UI/UX** - While easy for beginners, recent updates have degraded interface quality, introduced device optimization issues, and left the visual design feeling outdated.

## Quotes
- "In intraday when we booked profit it's hangs and buffer and goes profit into loss."
- "Money is cut without any reason, this is a very wrong system."
- "update but not for good, but for degradation. Extreme decrease in indicators."

## Actions
- Prioritize a dedicated sprint to address critical app performance bugs, focusing on freezing, order execution, and data accuracy to prevent financial losses.
- Conduct a comprehensive review of the fee structure, enhancing transparency and clearly communicating all charges to users.
- Initiate user research to understand specific pain points regarding post-update UI degradation and outdated design, planning iterative improvements.
