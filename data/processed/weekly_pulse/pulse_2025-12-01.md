# Weekly Product Pulse: Stability, Fees, and Support Insights (Dec 1-7)
_Week: 2025-12-01 - 2025-12-07_

This week's pulse highlights user concerns primarily around app stability, specifically frequent glitches and critical failures. Fees and charges continue to be a significant pain point impacting user profitability and platform loyalty. Customer support feedback remains mixed, indicating both excellent service and areas for improvement.

## Themes
- **Glitches & Bugs** - Negative sentiment persists regarding frequent system glitches, inaccurate data displays, and critical app failures impacting overall usability and trust.
- **Fees & Charges** - Users express strong dissatisfaction with perceived high brokerage fees, automatic deductions, and the significant portion of profits consumed by various charges, driving some to consider alternatives.
- **Customer Support** - Feedback on customer support is varied, with some users praising ease of understanding and smooth processes, while others report negative experiences, suggesting inconsistency in service quality.

## Quotes
- "I am unable to open my app it's not responding (Glitches & Bugs)"
- "if you get 100rs profit 50-60rs will be taxes. (Fees & Charges)"
- "worst service go to zerodha (Customer Support)"

## Actions
- Prioritize investigation and resolution of critical app failures and data display inaccuracies to restore user trust and functionality.
- Review the fee structure and communication around charges to ensure transparency and address user concerns regarding profitability.
- Implement a quality assurance program for customer support interactions to standardize service excellence and address negative feedback.
