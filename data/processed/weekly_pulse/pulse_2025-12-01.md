# Weekly Product Pulse: 2025-12-01 to 2025-12-07
_Week: 2025-12-01 - 2025-12-07_

This week's pulse highlights critical user feedback across three key areas. Users frequently encountered application glitches, including inaccurate data displays and unresponsiveness. Significant concerns were raised regarding high and unexpected fees, eroding trust and profits. Additionally, there is clear demand for advanced features like US stock trading and enhanced portfolio linking.

## Themes
- **Glitches & Bugs** - Users are frequently encountering critical application issues, from inaccurate data display to unresponsive apps and failed trade executions, indicating a significant negative impact on user experience and trust.
- **Fees & Charges** - Users express strong dissatisfaction with high and unexpected brokerage fees and taxes, which are significantly eroding profits and leading to perceptions of expense.
- **Feature Requests** - There is a clear user demand for advanced investing functionalities, including direct stock investing improvements, external portfolio linking, and US stock trading.

## Quotes
- "Glitches & Bugs: groww app is showing wrong candle of index chart."
- "Fees & Charges: too much CHARGES and TAXES. if you get 100rs profit 50-60rs will be taxes..."
- "Feature Requests: please add us stock feature in mobile app ."

## Actions
- Prioritize investigation and resolution of critical chart display inaccuracies and app unresponsiveness to restore user trust and reliability.
- Review current fee structures and communication, considering options to reduce perceived burden or clearly articulate value to users.
- Evaluate feasibility for integrating US stock trading and enhanced portfolio management features based on user demand.
