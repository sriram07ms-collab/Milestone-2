# Weekly Product Pulse: November 17-23, 2025
_Week: 2025-11-17 - 2025-11-23_

User feedback this week highlights critical issues impacting core platform functionality and financial transactions. Reports focus on significant application glitches affecting trade execution and data accuracy, alongside persistent challenges with payment processing and refund delays. Addressing these stability and reliability concerns is crucial for user trust.

## Themes
- **Glitches & Bugs** - User sentiment is highly negative due to pervasive application glitches, chart interactivity problems, and critical delays impacting trade execution and data consistency.
- **Payments** - User experience is mixed, with some positive feedback on transaction transparency, but significant concerns persist regarding withdrawal delays and unreceived refunds for rejected mutual funds.

## Quotes
- "Glitches: everytime when I switch to some other app and resume groww again the chart resets to the current position which is so annoying why can't you make it stay in one place where user has already placed it"
- "Glitches: When trying to exit, it doesn't exit, it hangs, and by the time the order confirms, the entire move is missed."
- "Payments: Mutual funds were rejected, and refunds were not received for three months."

## Actions
- Prioritize investigation and resolution of chart interaction issues and general application unresponsiveness, especially during critical trading actions (Glitches & Bugs).
- Conduct a thorough audit of data consistency mechanisms to address inaccuracies reported across various account balance and P&L displays (Glitches & Bugs).
- Streamline the refund process for rejected mutual funds and improve communication regarding expected timelines for withdrawals (Payments).
