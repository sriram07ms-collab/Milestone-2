# Weekly Product Pulse: Key User Feedback (Nov 17-23, 2025)
_Week: 2025-11-17 - 2025-11-23_

This weekly product pulse highlights critical user feedback from November 17-23, 2025. Key areas of concern include persistent technical glitches, increasing dissatisfaction with fees, and significant challenges accessing effective customer support, impacting overall user experience and platform trust.

## Themes
- **Glitches & Bugs** - Users express significant frustration with pervasive technical issues, from chart malfunctions and order execution problems to data inconsistencies, severely impacting platform reliability.
- **Fees & Charges** - A negative sentiment persists regarding high and increasing charges, particularly brokerage fees, leading users to consider competitor platforms due to perceived unfavorable pricing.
- **Customer Support** - Users consistently report extreme difficulty connecting with and receiving effective assistance from customer support, resulting in unaddressed critical issues and declining user confidence.

## Actions
- Prioritize investigation and resolution of critical chart and order execution bugs to stabilize core platform functionality.
- Review current fee structures and conduct a competitive analysis to address user concerns about high charges and potential churn.
- Implement immediate measures to improve customer support accessibility and responsiveness, focusing on reducing wait times and improving issue resolution rates.
