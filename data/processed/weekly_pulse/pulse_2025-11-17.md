# Weekly Product Pulse: November 17 - 23, 2025
_Week: 2025-11-17 - 2025-11-23_

This week's pulse highlights user frustrations stemming from critical app glitches impacting trade execution and data accuracy. Concerns also persist regarding high and increasing platform fees, alongside significant issues with payment withdrawals and delayed refunds for mutual funds.

## Themes
- **Glitches & Bugs** - Users expressed significant frustration with frequent technical glitches, negatively impacting app refreshing, trade execution, and the accuracy of displayed data.
- **Fees & Charges** - Customer sentiment is negative regarding the platform's high and increasing charges, particularly brokerage, which is influencing decisions to consider competitor platforms.
- **Payments** - Users reported ongoing problems with fund withdrawals, incorrect deductions, and prolonged delays in receiving refunds for rejected mutual funds, indicating a need for system improvements.

## Quotes
- "Glitches & Bugs: Exit karne par exit nhi hota hang ho jata hai , aur jab exit kare tau order confirmation hota hai itne mai sara move nikal jata hai"
- "Fees & Charges: Brokrage Charges Little high.. Please Focus on this Issue.. Increase Day by day"
- "Payments: if some incorrect money is deducted, customer help does not assist. My 3 mutual funds were rejected and I haven't received a refund even after 3 months."

## Actions
- Prioritize the resolution of critical app stability issues, focusing on trade execution failures and data display inconsistencies (Glitches & Bugs).
- Conduct a competitive review of the current fee structure, especially brokerage rates, to address user concerns about high charges (Fees & Charges).
- Investigate and implement improvements to the fund withdrawal system to allow for greater flexibility and expedite mutual fund refund processing times (Payments).
