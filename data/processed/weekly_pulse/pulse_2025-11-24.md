# Weekly Product Pulse: 2025-11-24 to 2025-11-30
_Week: 2025-11-24 - 2025-11-30_

This week's pulse highlights critical user feedback concerning application stability and financial transparency. Users frequently report significant glitches impacting core functionalities and data accuracy. Concerns persist around high and sometimes incorrect brokerage charges. Customer support receives mixed feedback, with AI noted for efficiency but human support often criticized for delays.

## Themes
- **Glitches & Bugs** - Users express strong negative sentiment due to frequent application glitches, data inaccuracies, and crashes, severely impacting core trading functionalities.
- **Fees & Charges** - Negative sentiment is driven by perceptions of high brokerage fees and specific concerns about incorrect or excessive charges, influencing investment decisions.
- **Customer Support** - Feedback is mixed, with appreciation for the AI Voice Assistant's efficiency, but significant dissatisfaction stemming from slow response times and inefficient processes with human support.

## Quotes
- "Too much glitch, option chain not open properly sometime working sometimes not working."
- "your charges are soo costly"
- "not proper response and delay service"

## Actions
- Prioritize engineering efforts to address reported glitches, focusing on application stability, data accuracy, and critical trading functionality.
- Conduct a comprehensive review of brokerage and MTF interest charges, ensuring transparency and competitive pricing.
- Investigate and improve human customer support response times and efficiency, learning from the positive feedback on the AI Voice Assistant.
