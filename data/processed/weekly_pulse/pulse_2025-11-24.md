# Product Pulse: Nov 24 - Nov 30
_Week: 2025-11-24 - 2025-11-30_

This week's pulse highlights critical user concerns regarding financial discrepancies. Key issues include automatic negative balances, lost deposited funds, and frequent complaints about high and incorrect brokerage calculations, alongside unexpected deductions. Addressing these payment and fee transparency issues is paramount to user trust.

## Themes
- **Payments** - Significant negative sentiment surrounds payment processing, with reports of automatic negative balances and difficulties in tracking or recovering deposited funds.
- **Fees & Charges** - Users express strong dissatisfaction with fee transparency and accuracy, citing high brokerage rates and unexpected deductions.

## Quotes
- "Payments: "the balance is going into automatic minus, it is a fraud.""
- "Fees & Charges: "mtf interest related fraud""
- "Fees & Charges: "your charges are soo costly""

## Actions
- Initiate an urgent investigation into automatic negative balance reports and implement preventative measures to protect user funds.
- Streamline the process for tracking and recovering lost deposited funds, ensuring clear communication and faster resolution times.
- Conduct a thorough audit of all brokerage and associated charges to ensure accuracy, address discrepancies, and improve transparency for users.
