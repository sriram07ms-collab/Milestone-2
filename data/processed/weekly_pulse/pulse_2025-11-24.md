# Weekly Product Pulse: 2025-11-24 to 2025-11-30
_Week: 2025-11-24 - 2025-11-30_

This week's product pulse reveals critical user feedback centered on core platform functionality. Significant issues include widespread application glitches impacting user experience, concerns over high and unclear fees leading to distrust, and severe problems related to payment processing, including reported fund losses. Addressing these areas is crucial for user retention and satisfaction.

## Themes
- **Glitches & Bugs** - Users consistently report frustrating application instability, including freezing graphs, order execution glitches, and unexpected crashes, severely impacting reliability.
- **Fees & Charges** - Negative sentiment stems from perceived high brokerage and hidden charges, with users questioning the transparency and accuracy of various deductions.
- **Payments** - Critical issues with fund management, including reported loss of deposited money and difficulties with bank account updates, are creating significant user distress and trust concerns.

## Quotes
- "Glitches & Bugs: the graphs keep getting stuck."
- "Fees & Charges: brokerage charges jayada hai"
- "Payments: I lose my money in this app."

## Actions
- Prioritize investigation and resolution of reported application stability issues, focusing on graph updates and crash prevention.
- Conduct an immediate, comprehensive audit of all fees and charges to ensure transparency, accuracy, and clear communication to users.
- Form a dedicated rapid response team to address fund recovery complaints and improve the reliability of payment processing and bank account management features.
