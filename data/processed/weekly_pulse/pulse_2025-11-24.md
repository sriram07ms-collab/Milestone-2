# Weekly Product Pulse: Nov 24-30, 2025

This week's pulse shows user concern over core application stability, frequent glitches, and inconsistent financial data. High brokerage fees cause dissatisfaction. Customer support is mixed; AI is efficient, but human interaction is criticized for slowness.
